Bio

I am a computer scienec PhD student at Concordia university working under Prof. Ravanelli's supervison. I am working on deep learning for sequence processinga and Conversational AI and collaborating on SpeechBrain project. My main research interests are Conversational AI, Natural Language Processing and Multi Modality.

I was working as a ML and NLP research scientist at UKP Lab in TU Darmstadt working on "AI In Digital Mental Health" project. During my MSc studies, I have worked as a data science intern at Pioneer Natural Resource company working on anomaly detection algorithms for extremely rare events. I have also been working as a full stack developer at Dotin Company which is provider of SOA-based multi-channel software products for banks and financial services institutions.
